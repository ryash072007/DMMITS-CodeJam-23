Initial Major Experiments

Satellite Telecommunication Experiments Project (STEP) was the next step forward. A key objective of STEP was to conduct a system test of geosynchronous satellites for domestic communication, enhance capabilities, and gain experience in designing, fabricating, installing, operating, and maintaining a variety of ground segment facilities that would all result in INSAT's production and operation.

Experimental Satellite Communication Earth Station was one of the first major experiment conducted. It was conducted as a test to see if satellite tv communication was viable or not.

Following its success, Sattelite Instructional Television Experiment is setup to train 50000 science teachers across 2000 villages helping more than 200000 people. This experiment also had to use russian made rockets.
